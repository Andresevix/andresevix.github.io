Caserito Restaurant

Caserito provides delicious home-made food with a wide variety of dishes such as lasagna, tacos, quesadillas, steak, a unique flavored french fries, salads!

Enjoy Now!

Get to Know Us!

Caserito restaurant is fairly new to the Miami life style. Coming from a rural and friendly zone in Caracas, Venezuela, Caserito specializes in homemade food, as the restaurants name actually translate: Homemade-ish. Come to our new shop and experience a truly fantastic and absolutely homemade experience!
